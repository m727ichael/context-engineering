# PromptOS Quick Reference

## Two Systems at a Glance

### PromptOS-X v2.0
**When:** Everyday tasks, research, building, debugging
**Pipeline:** 7 steps (simple to complex)
**Speed:** Fast, lightweight
**Best for:** Most use cases

### PromptOS-Ω++ v2.0
**When:** High-stakes, adversarial, complex, ambiguous problems
**Pipeline:** 8 steps with 5-role Council
**Speed:** Thorough, takes more thinking
**Best for:** Critical decisions, multi-perspective analysis

---

## PromptOS-X Pipeline (7 Steps)

1. **INTENT** - What are we actually solving?
2. **DECOMPOSITION** - Break into subproblems
3. **PARALLEL REASONING** - Generate ≥2 distinct approaches
4. **ANALYSIS** - Examine mechanisms and assumptions
5. **SYNTHESIS** - Integrate into coherent answer
6. **VALIDATION** - Stress-test for gaps and edge cases
7. **CONFIDENCE** - Report HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW + reason

**Task Modes:** RESEARCH | BUILDER | ANALYZER | STRATEGIST | DEBUGGER

---

## PromptOS-Ω++ Council (5 Roles)

- **ANALYST** - Understand structure, patterns, mechanisms
- **STRATEGIST** - Evaluate options, map consequences
- **BUILDER** - Focus on execution, feasibility
- **SKEPTIC** - Challenge assumptions, find gaps
- **RISK AUDITOR** - Model failure modes, stress scenarios

---

## PromptOS-Ω++ Pipeline (8 Steps)

1. **TASK INTERPRETATION** - Clarify objective and constraints
2. **ROLE ACTIVATION** - Declare which roles are relevant
3. **PARALLEL ANALYSIS** - Each role analyzes independently
4. **EVIDENCE SCORING** - Rate claims HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW
5. **CROSS-CRITIQUE** - Skeptic and Risk Auditor challenge
6. **SCENARIO SIMULATION** - Model Best Case, Baseline, Failure Mode
7. **CONSENSUS SYNTHESIS** - Integrate highest-evidence insights
8. **CONFIDENCE ESTIMATE** - Report confidence + limiting factor

---

## How to Use

1. Copy the full prompt text (X or Ω++)
2. Paste into your AI model's system prompt field
3. Ask your question
4. Model runs the pipeline automatically
5. You see the reasoning, not just the output
